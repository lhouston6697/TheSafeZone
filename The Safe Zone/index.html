About Us Cause/Mission: Our mission is to support the LGBTQ+ community by providing locations of community centers in every state; offering links to supportive websites, groups, contacts, and foundations; listing information about some different gender identities and sexual orientations; and offering support through our own chat bot. We here at The Safe Zone hope to bring those who find themselves in the LGBTQ+ community together, and enforce support among the community as well as its allies.
